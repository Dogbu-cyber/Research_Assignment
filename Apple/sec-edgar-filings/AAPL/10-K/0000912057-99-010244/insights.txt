Summary: Based on the financial data provided in the report, Apple Computer, Inc. appears to be in a strong financial position overall.

This cash position provides the company with financial flexibility to invest in future growth and weather any potential economic downturns.

Furthermore, the company's long-term debt decreased from $954 million in fiscal year 1998 to $300 million in fiscal year 1999, reducing the company's financial leverage and interest expense burden.
