Net Income: $12,193 million

Operating Income: $18,161 million

Summary:
Microsoft's overall financial health appears strong, with solid revenue growth, profitability, and a strong cash position. The company reported revenue of $93,580 million, an 8% increase from the prior year. Operating income was $18,161 million, though this represents a 35% decrease from the prior year, primarily due to $10,011 million in impairment, integration, and restructuring expenses. 

The company's net income of $12,193 million, while down 44% from the prior year, still demonstrates the company's ability to generate substantial profits. Microsoft's cash, cash equivalents, and short-term investments totaled $96,526 million as of June 30, 2015, providing ample liquidity and financial flexibility.

The company's diverse business segments, including Devices and Consumer and Commercial, have contributed to its overall financial strength. The Commercial segment, in particular, has seen strong growth in areas like server products, cloud services, and enterprise software. This diversification helps mitigate risks and provides multiple avenues for future growth.

While the company faced some challenges, such as the impairment charges related to the Phone Hardware business, the overall financial data suggests Microsoft remains in a solid financial position to continue investing in its strategic priorities and delivering value to shareholders.
