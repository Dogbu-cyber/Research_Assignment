Microsoft's financial health appears to be strong based on the data provided in the report. The company reported a net income of $72,738 million and an operating income of $83,383 million for the fiscal year 2022, both of which represent significant increases from the previous year.

The company's balance sheet also appears to be in a healthy state, with $104.8 billion in cash, cash equivalents, and short-term investments as of June 30, 2022. This strong liquidity position provides the company with the financial flexibility to invest in growth initiatives, make strategic acquisitions, and return capital to shareholders through share repurchases and dividends.
